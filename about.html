Established 2013

The Institute

Indian Institute of Information Technology Vadodara (IIIT V) is established by the Ministry of Education (MoE), Government of India under Public Private Partnership (PPP) as an Institute of National Importance with a view to develop new knowledge in information technology and to provide manpower of global standards for the information technology industry.

The partners in this project are Government of India, Government of Gujarat, Gujarat Energy Research and Management Institute, Gujarat State Fertilizers and Chemicals Ltd and Tata Consultancy Services. The Institute was established in the year 2013.

The Government of Gujarat has lent support to the Institute by providing 62.5 acres of land at Dumad, Vadodara and an Academic Block at GEC, Gandhinagar.

Vision & Mission

Our guiding light towards excellence

Vision

To be a globally recognized institution of excellence in Information Technology education, research, and innovation that contributes to the socio-economic development of the nation.

Mission

  • To produce globally competitive IT professionals and researchers
  • To foster innovation and entrepreneurship
  • To develop strong industry-academia partnerships
  • To contribute to nation building through technology

Institute Timeline

Milestones in our journey of excellence

2013

Institute Founded

IIIT Vadodara established by MoE under PPP model as an Institute of National Importance.

2014

First Batch Enrolled

First batch of BTech students began their journey at the temporary campus in GEC Gandhinagar.

2017

IIIT (PPP) Act Enacted

Parliament passed the IIIT PPP Act, granting Institute of National Importance status.

2018

First Convocation

First batch graduated with flying colors, marking a milestone in the institute's history.

2020

Dumad Campus Land Allocated

62.5 acres of land at Dumad, Vadodara allocated for the permanent campus.

2024

Growing Excellence

Multiple programs launched including MTech, MCA, PhD. Research output and placements on the rise.
