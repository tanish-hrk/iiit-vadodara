About IIIT Vadodara

Indian Institute of Information Technology Vadodara (IIIT V) is established by Ministry of Education (MoE), Government of India under Public Private Partnership (PPP) model. It is declared as an Institute of National Importance by IIIT (PPP) Act 2017.

The institute focuses on cutting-edge research and education in Information Technology and allied areas, aiming to create knowledge-based leaders who can solve tomorrow's challenges today.
