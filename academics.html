B.Tech — Under Graduate

4-year undergraduate program through JEE Main via JoSSA/CSAB

🖥️ Computer Science & Engineering

Duration: 4 Years (8 Semesters)

Eligibility: JEE Main qualified, 10+2 with PCM

Intake: 165 seats

Core areas include Programming, Data Structures, Algorithms, DBMS, Networks, AI/ML, Software Engineering, and more.

📡 Electronics & Communication Engineering

Duration: 4 Years (8 Semesters)

Eligibility: JEE Main qualified, 10+2 with PCM

Intake: 66 seats

Focus on Signal Processing, VLSI, Embedded Systems, Communication Systems, IoT, and Microelectronics.

M.Tech — Post Graduate

2-year postgraduate program through GATE score

🔬 M.Tech in Computer Science & Engineering

Duration: 2 Years (4 Semesters)

Eligibility: GATE qualified with B.Tech/BE in CS/IT/ECE

Specializations: AI & Machine Learning, Data Science, Cybersecurity, Computer Networks

The program combines advanced coursework with research thesis, preparing students for industry and academia.

MCA — Master of Computer Applications

2-year professional program for computer application expertise

📱 MCA Program

Duration: 2 Years (4 Semesters)

Eligibility: NIMCET / Institute Level Entrance

Comprehensive software development curriculum covering Programming, Web Technologies, Mobile Apps, Cloud Computing, and Database Management. Industry-ready graduates for IT sector.
