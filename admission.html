Admission Process

Your step-by-step guide to joining IIIT Vadodara

1

Entrance Exam

Qualify JEE Main (BTech), GATE (MTech), NIMCET (MCA), or Institute Entrance (PhD)

2

Counselling

Participate in JoSSA/CSAB counselling for BTech or direct admission for other programs

3

Seat Allotment

Accept the seat allotment and pay the required admission fee

4

Document Verification

Complete document verification and submit originals at the institute

5

Enrollment

Complete enrollment formalities and begin your academic journey at IIIT Vadodara
